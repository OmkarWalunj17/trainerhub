Workflow

Streamlined Operations
in 3 Steps

01

Manager Posts Requirement

Managers define the training needs (Budget, Tech Stack, Mode) for their department via the portal.

02

Smart Assignment

Coordinators use the Auto-Assign Toggle to instantly match requirements with trainers based on expertise & budget.

03

Payment & Settlement

Managers pay securely via Razorpay. Coordinators handle the final offline settlement with trainers.

Key Features

Built for Efficient
Training Coordination

Smart Auto-Assign

Enable the toggle to automatically assign trainers whose expected salary fits within the manager's budget buffer.

Requirement Tracking

Managers can track their request status from Open to Assigned, and finally to Closed upon payment.

Expected Salary Data

Coordinators store private salary expectations for every trainer to ensure profitable matching logic.

Real-Time Status

See live updates on payment status (Pending, N/A, Completed) directly on the dashboard.

Role-Based Access

Separate workflows: Managers focus on requirements & payments; Coordinators focus on trainer inventory & profit.

Secure Payments

Integrated Razorpay gateway ensures funds are collected from managers securely before closing requirements.
